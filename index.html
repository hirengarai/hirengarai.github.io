About

Known as Hiren (হীরেন) [pronounced: /hiːrɛn/ ]. I am currently a Research fellow advised by Prof. Thomas Peyrin at SPMS in NTU Singapore. Currently I am exploring automatic tools for cryptanalysis along side with quantum computing.
