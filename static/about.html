1995 — 2005

Where It Started

Born and brought up in Mumbai. Crowded trains, monsoon floods, and the constant hum of the city. Early fascination with puzzles and numbers started here — probably from too many hours staring at railway timetables.

2005 — 2013

School Days in Bolpur

Moved to Bolpur for schooling — a quieter life near Shantiniketan. Red soil, sal trees, and lazy afternoons. This is where mathematics stopped being homework and started being interesting.

location_on Bolpur, West Bengal
2013 — 2018

College & First Research Steps

Undergraduate at Bolpur College, then B.Ed at Visva-Bharati. First exposure to research culture and the idea that you could spend your life thinking about problems. Got hooked.

location_on Bolpur, West Bengal
2019 — 2024

PhD at ISI Kolkata

Deep dive into cryptography at Indian Statistical Institute. Late nights debugging code, early mornings reading papers, and the thrill of finding a new attack. Coffee consumption reached historic highs.

location_on Kolkata, India
2024 — Now

Research Life in Singapore

Research Fellow at NTU Singapore. Still breaking ciphers, still drinking too much coffee. New city, new challenges, same curiosity. The journey continues.

location_on Singapore
